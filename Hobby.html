    What is Karate?

    Karate is a martial art that originated in Okinawa, Japan. It focuses on striking techniques such as punches, kicks, knee strikes, and elbow strikes. Karate practitioners also learn blocking and parrying techniques to defend against attacks.

    Benefits of Practicing Karate

    • Improves physical fitness and strength
    • Enhances mental discipline and focus
    • Boosts self-confidence and self-esteem
    • Teaches self-defense skills
    • Promotes respect and humility
